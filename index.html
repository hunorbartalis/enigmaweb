Who are we?

Enigma is a community of people brought together by the passion for AI and Cognitive Science. We believe that the best ideas arise when everyone feels confident enough to take the responsibility to find personal solutions to problems.

Since 2023, we are the official study association for the Department of Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence at Tilburg University. We organize a variety of social and educational events to help our members learn, grow, and connect with others.

From workshops and guest lectures to hackathons and research presentations, through social events and open debates, we offer endless opportunities for personal and professional development, while impacting the society around us.

Our mission is to build a legacy which unites people beyond the time and space boundaries of a university experience. A network which lasts more than a lifetime.

Activities

We organize various activities for both social and educational purposes. They range from panel discussions and symposiums to field trips, fairs, and bootcamps. We also help organize yearly events with the university like our yearly kick-off and the career week.

If you are interested in organizing and activity with us, do not hesitate to reach out through the contact form below!

Membership

Enigma takes a unique approach to the Study Association gig as a whole, not the least when it comes to membership. That is why our membership is free and open to anyone, even those that study other programs!
